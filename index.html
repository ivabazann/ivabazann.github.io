Biography

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a research assistant at the University of Memphis, working in the Earthquake Modeling and Visualization Lab. My main research projects focus on crustal deformation and tectonic processes. I'm particularly interested in understanding how earthquakes impact the stress state of surrounding faults and magmatic systems. My work aims to model their responses from short to long-term perspectives, shedding light on the complex interactions that drive Earth's dynamic systems.
